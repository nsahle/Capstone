Key Finding: A Targeted, Not Broad, Formulary Change

GLP-1 prior authorization rates surged from 7% in 2020 to ~100% in 2025 — a 14-fold increase concentrated between 2023 and 2024. Insulin, SGLT-2, and DPP-4 inhibitors remained below 3% throughout. This is a class-specific policy response to GLP-1 adoption as weight-loss treatments, not a systemic tightening of diabetes formularies.
