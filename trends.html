Key Finding: A Targeted, Not Broad, Formulary Change

GLP-1 receptor agonist prior authorization rates increased from 7.0% in 2020 to ~100% in 2025 — a 14-fold increase concentrated between 2023 and 2024. Meanwhile, DPP-4 inhibitors, insulin, and SGLT-2 inhibitors remained below 3% throughout the study period. This pattern indicates a class-specific formulary response to GLP-1 adoption as weight-loss treatments, rather than a systemic tightening of formulary access for diabetes medications broadly.
